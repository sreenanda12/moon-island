Our Story

Moon Island was born out of a profound love for the untouched tranquility of nature. Here, the gentle rustle of coconut leaves and the subtle ripples of the backwaters replace the noise of the city, offering a timeless escape into Kerala's magnificent landscapes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to immerse you in authentic Kerala life, allowing you to disconnect from the world and reconnect with serenity. Every experience is carefully crafted to honor the local culture and the pristine backwater environment.

Experience Philosophy

We believe traveling is not just about visiting places; it's about the feelings you take back home. From exhilarating kayaking adventures to the comforting warmth of a traditional homestay meal, our philosophy centers on providing meaningful, unforgettable experiences that speak to the soul.
