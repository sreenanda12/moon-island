Our Story

Moon Island – A Hidden Island of Calm

Moon Island was born out of a profound love for the untouched tranquility of nature. Here, the gentle rustle of coconut leaves and the subtle ripples of the backwaters replace the noise of the city.

Our mission is to immerse you in authentic Kerala life, allowing you to disconnect from the world and reconnect with serenity. Every experience is carefully crafted to honor the local culture and the pristine environment.

Our Philosophy

“We offer a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation...”

We believe traveling is not just about visiting places; it's about the feelings you take back home. From exhilarating kayaking adventures to the comforting warmth of a traditional homestay meal, our philosophy centers on providing meaningful, unforgettable experiences that speak to the soul.

A Visual Journey

The Art of Paddling

Silently glide through intricate networks of wild canals. It is a meditative journey where the only sounds are bird calls and water meeting wood.

Authentic Village Life

Walk the paths of the locals. Witness the timeless art of coir making, traditional leaf weaving, and uncover the vibrant spirit of our community.

Golden Hours & Comfort

End your days watching majestic sunsets paint the skies orange, followed by a restful retreat in our boutique homestay filled with Kerala's heartfelt hospitality.
