🚣‍♂️ Our Activities

🌊

Kayaking Through Narrow, Wild Canals

Experience the untouched side of nature as you paddle through peaceful, narrow canals surrounded by lush greenery. This is perfect for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts who seek a close connection with the backwaters.

Highlights:

  • Calm and scenic routes
  • Safe and guided experience
  • Ideal for both beginners and experts
🚤

Shikkara Boating Ride

Relax and enjoy a traditional shikkara boat ride through the serene backwaters. Glide through beautiful waterways while witnessing local life and stunning landscapes.

Highlights:

  • Comfortable seating
  • Perfect for families and couples
  • Sunrise and sunset rides available
🚶‍♂️

Village Walk Experience

Step into the heart of local life with our guided village walk. Explore traditional homes, meet friendly locals, and experience the culture, lifestyle, and daily routines of the village.

Includes:

  • Coconut leaf weaving & Coir making
  • Toddy tapping & Toddy tasting / shop visit

Highlights:

  • Authentic cultural experience
  • Meaningful local interactions
  • Great for photography lovers
🏡

Homestay Experience

Stay with us and feel at home in the lap of nature. Our homestay offers a peaceful environment with traditional hospitality and modern comfort.

Features:

  • Clean and comfortable rooms
  • Authentic Kerala-style food
  • Friendly hosts with personalized service
  • Surrounded by nature and backwaters
